EXT. CLIFFSIDE - NIGHT

The wind howls against the jagged rock face. Rain lashes down in horizontal sheets. Below, the dark ocean surges with violent, rhythmic intensity.

ELIAS (40s, soaked to the bone) stands at the precipice. He clutches a weathered leather satchel to his chest as if it were a shield.

ELIAS
(shouting over the storm)
You can't take it! It doesn't belong to the waves!

He takes a step back. A jagged piece of rock crumbles under his boot and falls into the abyss.

VOICE (O.S.)
Everything belongs to the waves eventually, Elias.

Elias spins around. Emerging from the veil of rain is a FIGURE in a heavy yellow slicker.

The figure raises a flare gun. The red light reflects in Elias's terrified eyes.
